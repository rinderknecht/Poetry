\poemtitle{}

\settowidth{\versewidth}{like that afternoon}

\bigskip

\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
  In this waltz \\
  of oblivion \\
  and regrets

  I step forwards \\
  and I forget \\
  all the lessons \\
  I swore to remember

  You step backwards \\
  and you regret \\
  learning so much \\
  about me

  Come

  The third beat \\
  is not here yet \\

  we don't have to repeat \\
  the same mistakes

  spinning \\
  against each other

  Let us stand still \\
  like that afternoon \\
  in the pouring rain \\
  when I kept you warm \\
  in my arms
\end{verse}
