\poemtitle{}

\settowidth{\versewidth}{--- Let's make French toasts!}

\bigskip

\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
  What a waste \\
  I tell myself

  This whole bread \\
  stale \\
  which you forgot \\
  at the back of the cupboard

  I remember the loaf \\
  warm and tender \\
  like a promise \\
  of young love

  and

  --- Let's make French toasts! \\
  you say
\end{verse}
