Christian Rinderknecht translated Pablo Neruda's _Veinte poemas de
amor y una canción desesperada_ into French (Gallimard, Paris,
1998). He collected and translated poems by Paul Valéry into Spanish
(Ediciones Rilke, Madrid, 2016) as _Las granadas_. He translated
Gustavo Adolfo Bécquer's _Rimas_ into French (L'Harmattan, Paris,
2024). He published his own poems in English under the title _Running
On_ (2024). He gathered and translated a short anthology of poems by
the Hungarian poet Attila József into Spanish and French (2026).
