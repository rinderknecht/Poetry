Attila József is one of the most famous poets of Hungary. His
relatively short life in the first half of the 20th century was one of
hardships and despair, but also profound visions of beauty, and
intense feelings of love. József's most famous poems tend to be dark,
but, in this tiny anthology (thirty poems), we also selected different
tonalities, like contemplation, friendship, politics, humour, and
contentment.

Those learning Hungarian can download the companion glossary to the
book at
https://github.com/rinderknecht/Poetry/blob/master/Translations/Jozsef/English/glossary.pdf
